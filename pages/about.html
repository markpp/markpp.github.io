Who am I and what do I have to offer?

I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at Aalborg University's Visual Analysis and Perception (VAP) laboratory. Currently, I am taken with machine learning methods that can provide an alternative to the very successful supervised paradigm. The methods I am currently interested I call self-supervised, because the signal the learning algorithm needs is generated from the data using clever tricks. I want to emphasise that these methods only recently have begun to show promising results and are unlikely to usurp learning from human supervision using quality labels such as bounding boxes. I'm just old enough to have worked with the machine learning techniques that dominated before Deep Neural Networks took over everything.
