Research

Ph.D thesis

I successfully defended my Ph.D thesis "Applications of Vision and Robotics in Meat Production", in September 2020. The Ph.D is a result of a three year industrial Ph.D. fellowship at Danish Technological Institute.

Master's thesis

I received my M.Sc. in Vision, Graphics and Interactive Systems from Aalborg University in 2015, after having done research in Advanced Driver Assistance Systems as a visiting student at the Laboratory for Intelligent and Safe Automobiles at the University of California, San Diego.
