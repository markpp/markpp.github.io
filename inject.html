Injection

Posted on Mon 23 January 2017 in projects

The brine injection problem was selected as the basis for a "quick and dirty" project where an adaptiv method could be applied. The project was to be confined to approximatly 3 month, this means that it is nessecary to significantly deliminate the purpose and make wide use of "hand held" solutions in order to quickly try out ideas.

Process description

Brine is pumped in to a wide range of meat before packaging in order to mature, tenderize and enhance flavour. The brine consists of water and a mixture of salts, nitrate and in some cases sugar or various types of flavoring.

Initial experiement

An inital experiment was conducted in order to investigate the effect of the two primary proces parameters; brine pressure and injection speed, as well as determine the possibilities for visually measuring the gain using 3D sensing.
